Provider Count Fixed Broadband (25 Mbps/3 Mbps) as of June, 2016

About

This map shows the number of providers offering residential broadband services of at least 25 Mbps download and 3 Mbps upload. The graph at right shows the population associated with each of these categories across a number of broadband speeds.

Enter your address to zoom to that location to see the number of providers that offer these residential services in your area. Tribal lands and urban areas can be displayed by clicking the appropriate box in the legend. Click on the symbol in the upper left hand corner next to + and – to change the view from a street view to a satellite or a terrain view. Zoom out to see whether the number of providers differs in the areas surrounding your address.

Form 477

For more information about the data underlying this map and how it is collected, please visit:

  • Fixed Broadband Deployment Data from FCC Form 477
  • Form 477 Resources
